Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Data Handling (The Important Part)

Trivox operates on a Client-Side First architecture. When you use our CSV Cleaner or Conversion tools, your files are processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. We do not upload, store, or view your files on our servers.

2. Analytics

We use anonymous analytics to track page views and button clicks to improve user experience. No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is collected.

3. Cookies

We use local storage to remember your dark mode preference. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

4. Contact Information

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@trivox.com.
